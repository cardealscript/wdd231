🏷️ Site Name

Name

CapitalFlow

Reason

"Capital" refers to financial assets and money management — a term widely recognised in formal finance and economics. "Flow" represents the movement of money: income coming in and expenses going out. Together, CapitalFlow conveys a professional, modern personal finance tool that helps users understand and control their financial flow on a monthly basis.

Optional Domain

capitalflow.finance

🎯 Site Purpose

CapitalFlow is a client-side personal finance management website that helps individuals track their monthly income and expenses, monitor their remaining balance, and make informed financial decisions. The site provides:

  • A Dashboard to register and categorize income and expenses, view real-time balance summaries, and visualize spending through interactive monthly charts.
  • A Currency Converter powered by ExchangeRate-API, enabling users to work with multiple currencies in real time.
  • A Financial Tips page offering curated advice on budgeting and saving, loaded dynamically from a local JSON file.
  • A live clock and date display showing the user's local time and timezone using JavaScript's native Date API.

All user data is persisted client-side via localStorage, ensuring a seamless experience across sessions without requiring a server or login system.

💬 Scenarios

The following questions represent what a typical CapitalFlow visitor might ask:

"How much money do I have left this month after all my expenses?"
"I receive my salary in USD but spend in MZN — how can I track my expenses in both currencies?"
"How can I see which category I am spending the most money on each month?"
"What practical tips can help me reduce unnecessary spending and save more?"

  • index.html — Landing page with hero section, live clock, balance summary cards, monthly chart preview, and currency converter widget.
  • dashboard.html — Full transaction manager: add income/expenses by category, view full transaction table, monthly chart built with Canvas API, and filter by category.
