CapitalFlow — Site Plan

WDD231 Individual Website Project — Cardeal Felisberto

1 Site Name

Name

CapitalFlow

Reason

"Capital" is a formal financial term referring to money and assets. "Flow" represents the continuous movement of income in and expenses out. Together, CapitalFlow communicates a professional, dynamic tool for monitoring personal financial health in real time — without requiring any account or installation.

Optional Domain

capitalflow.finance

2 Site Purpose

CapitalFlow is a client-side personal finance management website that helps individuals track monthly income and expenses, monitor their net balance, and make informed financial decisions. It provides:

  • A landing page with a live clock (HH:MM:SS + timezone) and a real-time currency converter using the ExchangeRate-API, with a historical chart powered by the Frankfurter API.
  • An interactive dashboard to add and categorize transactions, view balance summary cards, and visualize monthly spending with a bar chart built using the native Canvas API.
  • A financial tips page with 20+ curated tips loaded dynamically from a local JSON file, filterable by category and level.
  • All data persisted locally via localStorage — no server, no login, no data collection.

3 Scenarios

Questions a typical CapitalFlow visitor might ask:

"How much money do I have left this month after recording all my expenses?"
"I earn in USD but pay rent in MZN — can I see the current exchange rate and convert amounts?"
"Which spending category is consuming the biggest portion of my monthly budget?"
"What practical tips can help me build an emergency fund and reduce unnecessary spending?"

5 Typography

IBM Plex Sans — Weight 700 — Headings, site name, section titles, card titles
CapitalFlow — Take Control of Your Money Flow
IBM Plex Sans — Weight 400 / 500 — Body text, paragraphs, descriptions, table data
Track your monthly income and expenses with clarity. CapitalFlow helps you stay in control of your personal finances every single day — without any account required.
IBM Plex Sans — Weight 600 — Labels, badges, buttons, navigation links, form labels
Total Income  ·  Add Transaction  ·  All Categories  ·  Open Dashboard

A single typeface family — IBM Plex Sans — is used throughout the entire site. Its five weights (300, 400, 500, 600, 700) provide sufficient typographic hierarchy without introducing visual inconsistency. The font is served via Google Fonts and reflects the fintech and institutional design language of organisations like IBM, Stripe, and Bloomberg.

6 Site Pages

index.html — Landing page: hero, live clock, feature cards, currency converter with historical chart.
dashboard.html — Transaction manager: add transactions, balance summary cards, monthly bar chart (Canvas API), category breakdown, filterable transaction table.
tips.html — Financial tips: 20+ tips from local JSON, category chips, search, level filter, modal with full detail.
contact.html — Contact form: first name, last name, email, subject, message, newsletter checkbox. Validates before submitting.
form-confirmation.html — Displays submitted form data via URLSearchParams. (Does not count toward 3-page requirement.)
attributions.html — Credits for icons, fonts, APIs, and tools. (Does not count toward 3-page requirement.)
