About Me

My name is Cardeal Felisberto, born on February 28, 2002, in Maputo, Mozambique. My native language is Portuguese, and I have acquired English through dedicated learning including training and cultural exchange at the U.S. Embassy in Maputo, where my passion for education abroad, especially in the United States, was truly born.

By a great blessing, I discovered the BYU-Pathway Worldwide program in 2025 and completed PathwayConnect. That experience opened the door to my Bachelor's Degree in Software Development. I have already completed 5 courses, Introduction to Programming, Programming with Functions, Programming with Classes, Web Fundamentals, and Dynamic Web Fundamentals. I am currently taking my 6th and final course, Web Frontend Development I, which will complete my first certificate in Web and Computer Programming!

I am a future Software Developer, deeply passionate about technology and creativity. Lately I have been exploring UX/UI Design and falling in love with the art of building beautiful and functional web experiences. Every day I grow closer to my dream of becoming a Web Frontend Developer and this is just the beginning!
